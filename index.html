Hi, I'm Avril Loufrani,

I offer consulting for women's and children's wear, I guide brands from creative vision through production, creating distinctive collections that are timeless, responsible, and market-ready.

With over 15 years of experience in luxury ready-to-wear across Europe and the US, I have held Head Designer roles at renowned houses including Courrèges, Lanvin, Balenciaga, Missoni, and 3.1 Phillip Lim. Today, I combine this high-end expertise with an entrepreneurial mindset as the Co-founder and Creative Director of Club Trésor, a premium children’s fashion brand focused on creative design and sustainability.

I'm currently accepting select consulting engagements in creative direction, collection design & development, and brand strategy for FW26 and beyond.
